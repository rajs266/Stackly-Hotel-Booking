FAQ

Quick Answers

Common questions answered before you even ask.

What is the official address of Stackly Hotel?
MMR Complex, Chinna Thirupathi, near Chinna Muniyappan Kovil, Salem, Tamil Nadu 636008. We're located 15 minutes from Salem Junction and 45 minutes from Salem Airport.
How do I create an account on Stackly?
Click Get Started on any page, choose your role (Guest, Staff, or Admin), and complete the signup form. Verify your email, and you're ready to book or manage operations.
Can I manage my bookings online?
Absolutely! After signing in, visit your Dashboard to view upcoming stays, modify dates, request upgrades, download invoices, and access exclusive member offers.
Is early check-in available?
Subject to room availability. Standard check-in is 2 PM. Early check-in (from 10 AM) can be requested during booking or by calling reception 24 hours prior. Additional charges may apply.
What is your cancellation policy?
Free cancellation up to 48 hours before check-in. Cancellations within 48 hours incur a charge equivalent to one night's stay. No-shows are charged the full booking amount.
Do you offer airport transfers?
Yes! Complimentary pickup and drop from Salem Airport for Suite bookings. Standard room guests can book transfers at ₹800 per trip. Advance booking recommended.

Still Have Questions?

Our team is available 24/7 to assist you. Call, email, or WhatsApp — we respond within the hour during business hours.
