About Stackly

Boutique hospitality rooted in Salem — where comfort meets nature, and technology enhances every touchpoint.

Our Story

More Than a Hotel

Stackly was born from a simple vision: to create a sanctuary where modern travellers could disconnect from chaos and reconnect with nature — without sacrificing luxury or convenience.

Nestled in the serene Chinna Thirupathi region of Salem, our resort blends traditional Tamil hospitality with cutting-edge digital experiences. From seamless online booking to smart room features, we've reimagined what a hotel stay should feel like in 2026.

Journey

Our History

2001

The Beginning

Stackly opened as a modest 12-room guesthouse near Chinna Thirupathi, founded by Rajesh Kumar with a dream of bringing world-class hospitality to Salem.

2010

Major Expansion

Expanded to 50 rooms with the addition of a swimming pool, restaurant, and conference facilities. Became the preferred venue for Salem corporate events.

2018

Digital Transformation

Launched online booking platform, mobile app, and smart room technology. First hotel in Tamil Nadu to offer keyless entry and AI-powered concierge.

2026

Today & Beyond

76 rooms, 200+ staff, 50,000+ happy guests annually. Committed to carbon neutrality by 2028 and expanding to Coimbatore and Madurai.
