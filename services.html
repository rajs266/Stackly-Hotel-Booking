What We Offer

Resort Facilities

Meticulously maintained spaces designed for relaxation, wellness, and celebration.

🏊
Infinity Pool
A stunning rooftop infinity pool with panoramic views of the Salem hills. Heated water, poolside cabanas, and a juice bar. Open 6 AM to 10 PM daily.
🍽️
Fine Dining Restaurant
Award-winning chefs serve authentic South Indian cuisine and international flavours. Farm-to-table ingredients, open kitchen, and private dining rooms available.
💆
Ayurvedic Spa
Full-service wellness centre with traditional Ayurvedic treatments, Swedish massage, steam room, and aromatherapy. Certified therapists and organic oils.
🏋️
24/7 Fitness Centre
State-of-the-art gym with Life Fitness equipment, free weights, yoga studio, and personal trainers on request. Complimentary towels and protein shakes.
🍸
Sky Lounge & Bar
Premium rooftop bar with artisan cocktails, fine wines, and sunset views. Live music on weekends. Happy hours 5 PM – 7 PM.
🎭
Event & Banquet Halls
Three versatile halls accommodating 50 to 500 guests. Perfect for weddings, conferences, and celebrations. Full AV setup and catering included.
🎠
Kids Club
Supervised play area with games, crafts, and outdoor activities for children aged 3–12. Babysitting services available on request.
🚗
Valet & EV Parking
Complimentary valet parking for all guests. Secure covered parking with EV charging stations. Airport transfers available 24/7.
